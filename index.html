Life can be tough at times

Just like physical health, mental health is a core part of being human, and it influences everything. Our mental health and wellbeing impact our decisions, relationships, careers, overall lives, and quality of life. Every. Day.

And life can be tough at times.

While there is no secret formula, understanding key concepts and patterns can be invaluable to you.

Once you know these core concepts and patterns, the information and knowledge can benefit you for life.

Course overview

Practical Psychology And Your Everyday Life

Hi, I'm Mari Cairns

I am a licensed clinical psychologist with over two decades' experience in public and private healthcare in England. In 2021, my life changed when I lived through PTSD myself. As with many life-changing experiences, it was only then that I truly understood and felt psychology's real-life impact.

Developing this course was my way to pay it forward. I designed and built it based on thousands of hours of clinical experience. The course distils key practical concepts and patterns, with a focus on fundamental things that impact your mental health and wellbeing.
