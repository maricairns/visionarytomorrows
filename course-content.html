Course overview

Practical Psychology And Your Everyday Life

Course • 49 Lessons

Designed and developed by a licensed clinical psychologist, this course distils core concepts and patterns into a 3-hour series of short videos to support your mental health and wellbeing.

You'll learn what to look out for, understand how key processes work, and identify potential harms sooner. The information and knowledge is transferable across all areas of your life.

What to expect from the course

The online course is divided into four main interconnected sections

SECTION 1

Practical Psychology And Your Everyday Life

Ready to invest in your mental health and wellbeing, but unsure where to begin?

The course covers core concepts and patterns that impact your everyday mental health and wellbeing.

Learn what to look out for, and spot what others often miss. The information and knowledge can benefit you for life.

SECTION 2

Practical Psychology And Your Relationships

Is someone's behaviour inconsistent or leaving you confused at times?

Understand the powerful influence that others can have on your mental health and wellbeing.

Learn about core patterns, including how to identify toxic ones, and spot the subtle or hidden behaviours people often miss.

SECTION 3

Practical Psychology And Your Career

Want to learn about core workplace patterns and environments?

Recognise the impact your work has on your mental health and wellbeing, and the importance of trust and respect in healthy workplaces.

Learn what to look out for, how key processes work, and how to navigate a toxic work environment.

SECTION 4

Practical Psychology And Society

Do you feel overwhelmed by current affairs or the state of the world at times?

Understand how daily news, social media, and global events can impact your mental health and wellbeing.

Identify key societal themes, their interconnections, and the impacts on your mental health and wellbeing.
