Frequently Asked Questions

How will the online course support my mental health and wellbeing?

+

The online course was designed, developed, and built to distil core concepts and patterns. This information and knowledge can be applied in your everyday life. You'll learn what to look out for, understand how key processes work, and identify potential harms sooner. As a licensed/chartered clinical psychologist, I've spent thousands of hours meeting with people. These are the things people navigate.

How do I access the online course, and will I be able to revisit it?

+

The online course has instant access. After purchasing, simply create an account with your email and a password to log in and get started. Complete the course at your own pace, in your own space. You'll have lifetime access and can revisit the course whenever you like. It's also optimised with subtitles.

How do I get the course for people in my company?

+

Complete the get custom quote form, and I'll provide a quote based on the number of people. Each person will receive a unique access code for the online course.

What inspired you to create the online course?

+

In 2021, my life changed when I lived through PTSD myself. As with many life-changing experiences, it was only then that I truly understood and felt psychology's real-life impact. Developing this course was my way to pay it forward.

Can I give this course as a gift?

+

Yes, please email me and I will generate a gift card with a unique access code. Give the gift of mental health and wellbeing.

What does the logo symbolise?

+

The three mountains represent our past, present, and future. The path is purposefully set between the present and the future—between where you are now and where you're headed. And green is the international colour for mental health awareness.
