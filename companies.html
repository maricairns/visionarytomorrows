About Dr Mari Cairns

Dr Mari Cairns is a licensed clinical psychologist with over two decades' experience in public and private healthcare in England. She holds a doctorate in clinical psychology from the University of Oxford. In 2021, her life changed when she lived through PTSD herself and truly understood and felt psychology's real-life impact.

She developed the course as her way to pay it forward. The course was designed and built based on thousands of hours of clinical experience and distils key practical concepts and patterns that impact the mental health and wellbeing of the people in your company.
